Welcome to Cal's Student-to-Student Peer Counseling!
We provide free, confidential, one-on-one counseling to UC Berkeley students. Counselors are trained UC Berkeley students.
Book an Appointment
How it works
At your session, you can say and talk about what ever you want. We want to empathize and listen to your problems and hope that through meeting with us, you are able to feel less alone. At the end of every session, resources will be given to further assist you.
How it works
Our student-initiated and student-run group was established in 1967 to offer a unique service to the UC Berkeley community.
